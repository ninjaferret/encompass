The performance will include spoken text\, choreographed movement\, interaction with objects\, as well as recorded and live sound. \nAlongside Seita’s performance\, we invite the audience to engage with scientific\, artisanal and artistic mediations of maize – McClintock’s plant of choice – through the Reproductivities exhibition on display throughout the College.
