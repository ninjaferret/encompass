We want your feedback!

If you’d like to share your experiences and how you’ve been treated at a Safer Spaces-accredited organisation displaying our logo, or you’d like to provide general feedback about the Safer Spaces campaign, please write to us at saferspaces@encompassnetwork.org.uk.

We want to hear from you if you’ve had a particularly positive experience, or if you encounter consistently good practice. Help us to help other organisations by pointing out what makes you feel comfortable in places where you like to spend time, and where you buy products or services. This will help us to share positive examples of good practice with other organisations in the sector.

If you have a negative experience with a Safer Spaces-accredited business, please let us know by emailing saferspaces@encompassnetwork.org.uk. We will raise the issue of discriminatory practice or other negative treatment with the business or organisation in question and help them to address your concerns. While we cannot guarantee that negative behaviour will be resolved instantly, we’re committed to ensuring that your concerns are taken seriously. If serious concerns aren’t addressed, we reserve the right to withdraw our accreditation and prevent the business from using our logo.

If you’ve experienced a hate crime, please report this to the police. If you would prefer not to be identified, you can report the incident anonymously. You can find more information here.
