What we can do for you

Join our network

If you’re interested in the services we offer and you’d like to benefit from them, or if you have suggestions for how we can improve our offering, please join our network. We’d be delighted to hear from you!

Community funding

See our page on LGBTQ community funding (the ComPot) for more information on what we can offer.

Support for organisations

Encompass Network provides support for LGBT voluntary and community sector organisations and initiatives.

Two recent examples of promising initiatives that we’re proudly supporting are Queers in Shorts and the Pink Festival.

Your idea might not be quite as ambitious – or it might be even more ambitious! Either way, we’d be interested to hear from you to find out how we can support you.

We can provide support in the following ways:

  • Community funding via the ComPot – we have small amounts of start-up funding available for community initiatives and activities
  • Advice on organisational development,
  • Representation of the voluntary and community sector to the public sector, the private sector and the general public
  • Links to suitable funding schemes, and help with applications

To find out more about available funding, see the GRANTfinder UK website, or use the LGBT Consortium Funder Finder tool.

We also aim to keep Encompass Network members informed about local funding opportunities.

Networking meetings

In 2014, Encompass Network conducted a needs assessment for the LGBT community in Cambridge and the surrounding area. The assessment brings together key findings and recommendations from survey data and focus groups that were held throughout 2014.

In response to the needs identified within the LGBT community, we organise networking meetings for the LGBT community and public sector, business and voluntary and community sector organisations.

These meetings enable the organisations involved to liaise with one another around specific topics, have their voices heard, and coordinate relevant actions in response to needs in the community.

See the events page for upcoming networking meetings.
